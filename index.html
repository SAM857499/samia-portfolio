Case Study 01 · IoT Platform
ALTECH: Privacy-First Smart Home
MVP development for an offline-capable IoT automation platform
🔒 app.altech.sa/dashboard
Status
Pre-Launch (Q2 2026)
Team
4 Engineers, 2 Designers
Market
Saudi Arabia Residential
The Problem
Saudi homeowners faced privacy concerns around cloud-dependent IoT, connectivity gaps, and latency.
Key Decisions
  • Repositioned "offline-first" as a premium privacy feature based on 15+ interviews.
  • Architected local processing with cloud sync only when available (<100ms latency).
  • Scoped MVP to 4 device types and 8 core automations using RICE prioritization.
